What's VIX Digital ?

Meet us

We are a full service digital agency specialising in providing digital services to the public sector with teams in Warrington, London, Leeds and Sheffield. Our clients include NHS Digital, NHS England and Public Health England.

From complex CMS websites to bespoke product development, we work with our clients to build software that solves problems. Combining research, service design, software development and architecture, we work with clients throughout the lifecycle of their project from discovery through to building the live service and ongoing support.

Our multidisciplinary team spans designers to developers to information architects all with experience delivering projects in both the public and private sector. We use this experience to offer consultation services to help you create the right digital strategy for your team and service, as well as choosing the right technology for the job.

What we do ?

Wagtail specialists

We’re one of the leading Wagtail development agencies in the UK. We’ve worked with the NHS and Public Health England to create custom Wagtail services. Whether you’re an SME or a large organisation we can build website that serves your needs.

Service design

Our design process focuses on humans. We work with organisations to understand the needs of their users, enabling us to create bespoke, human-centred design. We work closely with with accessibility standards and GDS principles.

Applications & websites

We create pioneering web and mobile applications. Working with the latest technology to understand how it can help our clients. Take a look at our retainers to see how we can help you.

User research

We believe user research is a team sport and an ongoing process. Our projects start with a discovery phase, where we begin to understand your user needs using techniques such as prototyping workshops, persona development and user interviews.

Consultation

Our team has over 20 years of experience in all things digital from interoperability to content migration. We work with our clients to advise and help them develop digital strategies, implement the right tech and deliver digital projects that answer their organisational needs.

Hosting & support

We offer unique support packages with all of our software. Relieving organisations of the worries and pressures that come with taking care of live software. Our retainers provide the best ongoing support for projects.
